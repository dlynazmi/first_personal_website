Inspirational Quotes:

I was born and grew up in Malaysia. In October 2007, I went to University of Otago to further my PhD study in Computer Science.

I have travelled to many countries - Singapore, Brunei, Thailand, Indonesia, Japan, Egypt, Australia, England, Holland, Germany, Italy, Switzerland and France.

In this small city called Dunedin, I found my life. I had the chance to play volleyball with a great club. I am now learning Kung Fu Wing Chun which was my dream when I was a kid. I learnt to play guitar and composed a few songs on my own, as well as producing a few short films. I developed passion for photography and New Zealand is such a magnificent place to enhance my skill in this area.

I enjoy reading and writing novels. Back in Malaysia, I have published 7 Malay novels. I'm currently working on my first English novel, and hopefully one day I can fulfill my greatest dream, that is to be an international author.
